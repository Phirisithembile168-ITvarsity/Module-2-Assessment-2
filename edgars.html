EDGARS

Edgars is part of the Edcon Group. The first Edgars store opened its doors in South Africa in September 1929.

The very first store opened in the heart of Johannesburg. Today Edgars is proud retailer specialising in clothes, shoes, home appliances and home décor.

There are hundreds of stores around the country and the retailer also has a large online store too.Edgars also takes pride in their monthly Edgars specials. These specials can be found in their catalogue or on their website. The November Edgars sale is now on don’t wait, start shopping now!

Whether you’re looking for quality clothes or shoes or want to buy home appliances or décor from top brands, you can be sure that you will find it at Edgars. Edgars have always been an incredibly popular store in South Africa and shoppers support the chain for its high-quality products and excellent prices.

For anyone looking to save some money can browse the latest Edgars sale on their website. It is also a great idea to keep an eye on the Black Friday Edgars specials that run at the end of every year. The latest Edgars catalogue is available online and are packed with November Edgars sale items. Edgars is truly a store for everyone.
